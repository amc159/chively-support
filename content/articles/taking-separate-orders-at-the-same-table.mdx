## Overview

This guide covers Taking Separate Orders at the Same Table in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. 1. Setup on the Management Page.
2. On the management page, go to System settings/Location settings. On Purchase/Sale tab, click Edit on Sale and check Allow different groups to share the same table.
3. Tap on the table and click New Order.
4. A. Getting Payment from the Orders Page.
5. To get payment, go to Orders on the POS.
6. Click Pay on the order that’s being paid.
7. Click Pay to complete this order.
8. To get payment, go to Tables on the POS.
9. Select the desired table and order that needs to be checked out.
