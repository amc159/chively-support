## Overview

This guide covers Adding Fees for Issuing Gift Cards in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. On the management page, go to **System settings -> Location settings**.
2. Open the **Service charge** tab.
3. Click **Add service charge**.
4. Enter the fee details.
5. Enable **Apply to Issue gift card**.
6. If needed, enable automatic application at order time.
7. Click **Save**.
