## Overview

This guide covers Adding Fees for Issuing Gift Cards in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. 1. On the management page2. On POS.
2. 1. On the management page.
3. Go to System settings/Locations settings.
4. Press Add service charge. Fill in the information on the service charge and check Apply to Issue gift card. If you want to auto-apply this fee, check Auto apply. Then click Save.
5. On the Service charge tab, click Add service charge.
6. Fill in the information on the service charge and check Apply to Issue gift card. If you want to auto-apply this fee, check Auto apply when order. Then, click Save.
