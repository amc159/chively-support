## Overview

This guide covers How to Track Employee Time in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. On the management page, go to **Timekeeping** and set the feature to **Active**.
2. Open the **Settings** tab and update rules as needed for your workflow.
3. Select a date range and click **Load data**.
4. Review total working hours and daily time entries for each employee.
5. Keep the date range to a maximum of 31 days.
6. Select an employee row and click **Edit** to correct discrepancies.
7. Use **Add row** or **Delete row** to adjust clock-in/clock-out entries.
8. Enter a reason for each manual time edit.
9. If **Remind employees to clock in** is enabled, employees must confirm the **Clock in** prompt when they start a shift.
