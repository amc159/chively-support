## Overview

This guide covers How To Install Chively on Android POS Devices in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. Open the Amazon Store or click this link on your Android device.

3. Open the Files/Downloads folder on your device.

5. Enter your username and password, then click Next to start using the software.
6. Click the Search icon to search for your IP Address and then click Next.
