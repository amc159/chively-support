## Overview

This beta guide covers end-of-day reporting and close procedures so totals are reconciled before the business day is finalized.

## Before you begin

- Confirm all active checks/orders are closed or transferred.
- Confirm all terminals are online and synced.
- Confirm shift lead has reconciliation access.
- Confirm payment terminal batch/settlement timing for your processor.

## Steps

1. Open the end-of-day report for the target business date.
2. Review the summary totals: total sales, transaction count, and payment-method breakdown.
3. Check voids, refunds, and discounts to make sure exceptions are expected.
4. Compare cash expected versus counted cash and log any over/short amount.
5. Compare card totals with terminal batch totals when available.
6. Resolve or document mismatches before finalizing close.
7. Complete close and archive the end-of-day summary for accounting.
