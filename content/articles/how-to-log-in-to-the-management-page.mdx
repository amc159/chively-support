## Overview

This guide covers How to Log In to the Management Page in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. After customers successfully register for a Chively account on the sales management software, users will receive login information with administrator permissions.
2. Open a Web browser and access the Chively Backend login pageStep 2: Enter your Username and Password.
3. Click Login.
