## Overview

This guide covers Setting Up Flexible Item Pricing at Checkout in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. 1. On the admin page.
2. Go to System settings\Location settings.
3. In the Purchase/Sale tab, select Sales and click Edit. Check the box that says "Enable Set price pop-up for regular item with adjust price". Then, click Save.
4. Enter the price you want to enter for this particular order.
