## Overview

This guide covers How to Use the KDS Kitchen Screen in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. When accessing the KDS screen, a customer's order will appear on the kitchen screen as a prep ticket with a list of dishes to be prepared.
2. On the Current tab.
3. The first line of the order form shows the service type and salesperson, the time the order was created and sent to the kitchen.. The list of dishes is displayed by dish and the order of the menu group according to the.
4. As time passes, the color on each processing ticket will change from GRAY to ORANGE and then to RED to demonstrate how much time has passed. You can adjust the times in Settings/Undo/Transitions time.
5. For completed dishes, click on the dish on the ticket, and the program will display an additional dash to mark that dish as completed. You can also click on the ticket being prepared to mark it as completed when all it.
6. Click on a ticket that’s being prepared to mark the entire order as complete, or click on each item. When completed, the Current tab will display the status as Done, and the program will move the order to the Finish ta.
7. On the Schedule tab.
8. On the Sold out tab.
9. The default program will display both available and temporarily out-of-stock items, and will only pick up items set up in the Manual tracking section.
10. You have now successfully processed orders on the KDS digital screen using the Chively software!
