## Overview

This guide covers Setting Up a Flat Price Promotion in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. On the management page, go to Promotion. Click Add and select Flat price item.
2. Fill in the program nameStep 2: Select range of time for the promotionStep 3: Enter the flat price valueStep 4: Add items.
3. Add other conditions where applicable. Then click Save.

## Verification

- Confirm the change is visible in the expected Chively screen.
- Run a quick real-world test to ensure behavior is correct.
- If results are unexpected, double-check permissions and device/network status.
