## Overview

This guide covers Checking Employee Timesheets in Chively using a consistent, support-friendly workflow.

## Before you begin

- Confirm you can access the Chively Management Page or POS as needed.
- Make sure your account has permission to change these settings.
- Keep a test order, test payment, or test user available for validation.

## Steps

1. On the management page, select **Timekeeping**.
2. Review each employee's status, clock-in/clock-out records, and shift totals.
3. Update attendance records if corrections are needed.
4. To export a timesheet file, click **Export** on the Timekeeping screen.
5. Review the output file to confirm total working hours per employee.
